# Content Calendar & Publishing Roadmap

This document outlines the 12-month content strategy for Senury, including blog posts, guides, templates, and resources. Content is organized by quarter with specific topics, keywords, and publishing schedules.

3. **Digitalisierung** (Digital transformation)

## Content Production Workflow

### Weekly Process

**Monday: Planning**
- Review content calendar
- Check keyword rankings
- Adjust topics if needed

**Tuesday-Thursday: Creation**
- Research and outline (Day 1)
- Draft content (Day 2)
- Edit and optimize (Day 3)

**Friday: Publishing**
- Final review
- Schema markup
- Internal linking
- Publish and promote

### Monthly Process

**Week 1: Review**
- Analyze previous month's performance
- Update content calendar
- Plan resources

**Week 2-4: Production**
- Execute content calendar
- Batch similar content types

**End of Month: Optimization**
- Update old content
- Fix broken links
- Refresh outdated statistics

## Resource Requirements

### Team
- **Content Strategist**: 1 FTE (planning, editing)
- **Content Writer**: 1 FTE (German native, legal background preferred)
- **SEO Specialist**: 0.5 FTE (optimization, technical)
- **Designer**: 0.25 FTE (infographics, downloads)

### Tools
- **SEO**: Ahrefs or SEMrush ($100-300/month)
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics 4 (free)
- **CMS**: Next.js + Contentful or Sanity ($50-200/month)
- **Design**: Figma or Canva (free-$15/month)
- **Project Management**: Notion or Trello (free-$10/month)

### Budget Estimate
- **Personnel**: €80,000-120,000/year
- **Tools**: €3,000-5,000/year
- **Promotion**: €5,000-10,000/year
